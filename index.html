Hedging, also known as Hedge, is one of the risk management strategies. Hedges are used to reducing losses caused by market fluctuations (commodities, currencies, stocks).

Hedging is the process by which investors create an opposite trading order in order to avoid a situation in which the market moves in the opposite direction of the investor's prediction. Then, Hedging will make a profit by exploiting the price and time difference to create profitable value.
